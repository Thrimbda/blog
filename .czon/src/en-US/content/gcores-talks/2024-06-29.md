Broken Idasen

It used to be a rectangular table that could be raised and lowered, and in a distant age it would rise for a third of the time each hour.

Lately it seems to have tilted as it broke, propped up by books at a height of 140 cm, still serving as a desk for the monitor.

To follow in Lord Miquella's footsteps, I can only stand here for hours. This posture only heightens my impatience—it is said that the Lord of the Twin Moons was killed only three times by the standing Tarnished before being brutally executed.

Such exploration leaves no room for leisure; every second is spent running.

- 🥨 Tarnished Travelogue
