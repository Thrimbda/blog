After coming back, I did a citywalk with Playground, from Tian'an Thousand Trees to the Bund.

It was so refreshing, except that

When I left home it was 17°C, so I took off my thermal underwear and longsleeve, and put on a thin T-shirt with a thick shirt.
By the time I met up with Playground it was 12°C, and the wind was already howling. I was so cold I bought a padded jacket on the spot.
By the time we reached the Bund it was only 8°C. The two of us were shivering in the wind (or maybe just me?).

Regretted not checking the weather forecast yesterday.

But hey, this weather made the city feel surreal.

Playground's photography skills blow mine out of the water—Picture 1 was taken by me, Picture 2 by Playground.
