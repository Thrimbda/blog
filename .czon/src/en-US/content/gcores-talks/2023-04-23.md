After not playing games for two days, I still didn't feel like getting into it today. I remembered some old books I found after moving—two collections of English poetry: one is a Keats anthology that feels destined for me, and the other is Shakespeare's sonnets.

After gaming, I picked up the poetry book beside me and started reading. I struggled to read it fluently, but with the translation, I could understand it. Rereading it over and over, I found more pleasure in it. So it would be a huge crime not to share this Shakespeare poem.

The poem carries so much information that I almost want to cry when I close my eyes.

- 🧩A Fragment of Life
