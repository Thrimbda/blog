## About Attending Lectures

I haven't paid attention in class for over a year, mainly because I sleep too late, get too tired during lectures, and eventually just gave up. But today, on a whim, I decided to sit in the front row and listen. Surprisingly, it felt great.

## Reflections

I'm not sure if it's because I've accumulated a bit of experience lately or for some other reason, but I found that both teachers in 'Software System Analysis and Design' and 'Software Testing and Evaluation' mentioned some points I strongly agree with. It's like learning the hard way and then coming back to fix things.

+ The concept of a system
+ The importance of system design
+ The importance of requirements
+ Projects must have a contract signed!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Alright, I don't want to write anymore because I've forgotten the question I wanted to discuss.

Sad.
