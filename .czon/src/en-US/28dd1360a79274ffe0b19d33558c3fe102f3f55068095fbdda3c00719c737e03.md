# Ascending the Yuedu Pavilion

Peaks gather like a congregation, waves rage like fury, mountains and rivers guard the road to Tong Pass.

From the Yuedu Pavilion, the eight-hundred-li Qin Plain unfolds before the eyes:

Beholdâ€”the crisscrossing paths, the sounds of chickens and dogs, favorable weather, and a land blessed with outstanding people!

May we rise with the rooster's crow and carry forward the legacy!
