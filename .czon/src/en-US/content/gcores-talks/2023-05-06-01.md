During the May Day holiday, I returned to Xi'an but didn't have a chance to go to Xiaonanmen, nor did I get to eat my favorite Xinjiang-style pepper chicken.

So after coming back, I angrily learned a bunch of recipes and bought **six big chicken legs**! Added spices, boiled, shredded, and poured the prepared pepper sauce over it. Wow, it actually turned out pretty good, maybe only slightly inferior to the Xinjiang-style pepper chicken.

It's now part of Chef Wang's luxury menu, waiting to invite friends over to eat😋

Picture 1 is Chef Wang's pepper chicken with side dish of pan-fried asparagus and mushrooms (friends are welcome to come eat)
Pictures 2 and 3 are that Xinjiang-style pepper chicken (located at Xiaonanmen, welcome friends visiting Xi'an to go eat)
